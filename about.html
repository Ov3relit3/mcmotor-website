Who We Are

From Research Lab to Production Floor

MC Motor Technology Co., Ltd. (MC Motor) was founded by Shenzhen Peacock Talent scholars, a team of returning overseas PhDs, and leading technical experts from around the world. We are an advanced high-tech manufacturing enterprise focused on the design, R&D, manufacturing, and technical solutions for next-generation special motor systems.

Our products serve high-speed compressors, blowers, fans, pumps, heavy-payload electric UAVs, robotics, and new energy electric drive systems — representing the frontier of modern industrial electrification.

To date, MC Motor has secured nearly 100 core technology intellectual property rights worldwide, covering materials, processes, devices, and system design. The majority of these technologies have been successfully applied to our self-developed products, delivering measurable improvements in efficiency, reliability, safety, energy savings, lightweight design, and miniaturization.

Our mission is simple: replace outdated motor technology with better engineering. We don't just make motors — we solve drive-system challenges.

Our Team

World-Class Talent, Proven Results

MC Motor's R&D department has gathered a group of top-level engineering doctors, professors, and experts from both domestic and international institutions. This team provides complete, high-efficiency electric drive solutions across industries, with a powerful quality assurance capability for every product we develop and manufacture.

With nearly 20 years of accumulated experience in electromagnetic field-circuit computation and finite element analysis, our team ensures every motor design is optimized to world-class standards — from magnetic circuit topology to back-EMF THD minimization.

We follow a rigorous V-cycle development methodology that fully integrates structural, electromagnetic, control, and material engineering — resulting in motor products that achieve a qualitative leap in performance.
