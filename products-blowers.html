Core Blower Technologies

High-Efficiency PMSM

Self-developed permanent magnet synchronous motor with patented cooling technology. Replaces traditional induction motors for ~15% efficiency gain.

3D-Flow Impeller

Multi-curved blade design based on 3D flow theory. CFD-optimized for >80% impeller efficiency. Aviation-grade aluminum alloy construction.

Intelligent Control

Built-in self-designed PLC with anti-surge protection. Touch screen real-time monitoring of speed, temperature, voltage, and current. Automatic fault alarm and data logging.
